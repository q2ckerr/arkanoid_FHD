## Original Arkanoid Level 9 Layout (Arcade ROM)

Level 9 is known as a "checkerboard / alternating columns" pattern. The original arcade layout uses a **13-column grid** (x=0..12). The level consists of rows of alternating colored bricks with **silver bricks** forming a distinctive pattern.

Based on ROM data analysis, Level 9 features:
- A compact rectangular block of bricks in the upper portion of the playfield
- Rows of colored bricks (orange, cyan, green, red, pink, blue, yellow) with silver bricks interspersed
- The pattern is roughly 13 columns wide x ~8 rows tall
- Silver bricks (2 hits) are mixed in with single-hit colored bricks
